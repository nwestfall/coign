What is Coign?

Coign is a client-side AI agent SDK. You paste one <script> tag into your site, pick a model preset, and your visitors get a fully private, page-aware chatbot that runs on their own device. No server, no API key, no per-message cost.

Features

  • All inference happens in the visitor's browser via WebLLM
  • Three built-in tools: searchPage, getPageOutline, getElement
  • Five model presets from 400 MB to 4.5 GB
  • Conversation history in localStorage
  • Custom tool registration for integrators

Pricing

Coign is free and open source. The inference cost is paid by the visitor's device (GPU/CPU time and a one-time model download).

FAQ

What browsers are supported?
Chrome 113+, Edge 113+, Safari 18+, and Firefox with a WASM fallback.
Does my data leave the page?
No. All questions and answers stay on the visitor's device.
How big is the model download?
The demo preset (coign-tiny) is ~400 MB. Larger presets up to ~4.5 GB are available. Everything is cached after the first visit.
